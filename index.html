A high-level dialogue convening policymakers and industry leaders to examine how artificial intelligence is shaping the future of digital trade across APEC economies.

Since 2016, Digital Trade Policy Dialogues (TPDs) have provided a platform for exploring emerging trends, shared challenges, and opportunities in the digital economy.

As APEC economies increasingly leverage artificial intelligence (AI) to drive innovation and economic growth, this Dialogue builds on priorities outlined in the APEC AI Initiative and the 2025 APEC Digital and AI Ministerial Statement.

Discussions will focus on enabling a pro-innovation environment for industry-led AI growth, including cross-border data flows, regulatory interoperability, and cybersecurity.

The Dialogue will also highlight the role of public–private collaboration, featuring perspectives from APEC economies and industry leaders, including ABAC members.
